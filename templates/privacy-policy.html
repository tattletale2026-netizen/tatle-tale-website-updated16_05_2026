Privacy Policy

A legal disclaimer

Tatle tale is a community-led creative project based in Sheffield. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care, transparency, and respect.

1 Who we are

Tatle tale is an independent, not-yet-registered community project focused on intergenerational connection through storytelling and creative workshops.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is used, you can contact us at: tattletale2026@gmail.com

2 What information we collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our website, surveys, or workshops:

  • Name
  • Age or age group
  • Email address
  • Gender (optional)
  • Responses to surveys or workshop activities

We only collect information that is necessary to support participation in our community activities.

3 How we use your information

We use your information for community-focused purposes only, including:

  • Organising and managing workshops and events
  • Communicating with you about Tatle tale activities
  • Understanding community needs and improving our project
  • Research and planning, in anonymised or aggregated form where possible

We do not use your data for commercial purposes, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4 How we store and protect your data

We take appropriate steps to keep your information safe, including:

  • Storing data securely, such as password-protected systems
  • Limiting access to those directly involved in the project
  • Only keeping data for as long as necessary

While we take care to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We will do our best to respond quickly and responsibly to any issues.

5 Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Ask how your data is being used

To make a request, please contact us at tattletale2026@gmail.com.

6 Consent

By providing your information, you consent to it being used in line with this privacy policy.

Where appropriate, such as surveys or mailing lists, we will ask for clear consent before collecting your data.

7 Changes to this policy

As Tatle tale grows, this policy may be updated. Any changes will be shared on our website with a revised date.

8 Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact:

Tatle tale
Email: tattletale2026@gmail.com

We believe in creating spaces built on trust, care, and respect — and that includes how we handle your information.
